The streets of Nightmarket are a battleground for competing shopkeepers and would-be fashionistas, and Lark Street—famous for its many resellers and boutiques—is the most cutthroat street of them all.

Hanna Fjord greedy, conniving, the proprietor of Hanna Fjord’s Designer Boutique, has hired your talents to break into a rival shop belonging to Ellisa Halloran local, beloved, generous and steal the mock-ups Ellisa is planning to unveil at the upcoming the Art on Lark festival.

It’s just business, dearies. Ellisa should know her place and stick to what she does best—selling sewing supplies. Steal her gowns and jewelry, then throw them in the river or cut them into ribbons, I don’t care! Only my designs will be the star of the show!
Approach & Obstacles

Approach: The streetlamps outside Halloran's Haberdashery flicker and paint peels from the adjacent shops, one of which is boarded up. The haberdashery's recessed entrance is dark, as are the windows and the front door. It looks like it's been closed for a number of days.

  • Streetside: Though better days are in the past, Lark Street bustles with a flurry of shoppers and hawkers.

    • Triple-bolted front-door
    • A pair of Red Sashes stand guard by the loading dock
    • Broken second-floor windows face a back alley

  • Shop Floor: A guard dog sleeps between bolts of fabric and locked cabinets.

    • Low-hanging oil lamps
    • Crates of unsold bobbins, buttons, and cloth scraps
    • A trapdoor hides a stash of magical implements
    • Hastily-made tripwires

  • Design Room: Drawing tables and loose sketches fill the high-ceilinged back room.

    • Ellisa’s assistant, reading
    • Mock-ups on mannequins
    • Locked display cabinet of pattern books and grimoires
    • Heavy curtains over the windows keeps the room dim

Rumors: Ellisa Halloran died while working on her designs — Ellisa stole the designs herself
A Red Sash loan shark owns the shop’s debt — Ellisa’s assistant Rose mute, gifted is a Dimmer Sister

  • Rose barely lifts her eyes from her book. You can hear her voice in your head. "You were fools to come here." Mannequins and dress forms lurch towards you, aggressively.

    • A zealot hurls daggers in your direction

    • Rose snips off some of your fingers
    • The fighting attracts a Bluecoat patrol ( 6 )*

* For more information on clocks, check p. 15 of the Core Rulebook

Fashion is Danger: When touched or otherwise triggered, the gowns and jewelry suddenly spring to life, stuffing themselves onto you! They fit perfectly but won’t come off. Beyond attaching themselves to intruders, these statement pieces are cursed with more attributes that will impede your escape.
Dressed to Impress

Openly roll the tables below to come up with an appearance and a curse for each gown.
Roll twice for appearance and combine choice details, then roll once for a curse.

    Appearance

    • black velvet — wispy silk — gold trim
    • fabric wings — sheer drape — green veil
    • covered in writing — inlaid with jewels
    • gossamer — heavy fabric — long skirts
    • sleeveless — corset laces — slim tiara
    • feathered gold mask — knee-high boots

    Curse

    • Lying causes you Harm: Dazed (level 2)
    • The dress impatient, judgmental is talkative
    • Sequins chirp as you move your arms
    • Everyone thinks you're a famous Duke
    • When you make eye-contact, you sneeze
    • Animals follow you (e.g., rats, insects)

  • Siding with Ellisa

    Ellisa sighs heavily. She looks shaken but grateful.

    I can spare some Coin for you, in thanks for getting me out of that predicament. I might have some work for you in the future, if you’re in the protection business.

    She explains that while these dresses are her personal design, she had nothing to do with the curses.

  • Siding with Hanna

    I’m very pleased. Take your payment. Hanna Fjord will be the number one boutique stop after people see my designs at the festival!

    Hanna pays you and shows you her own designs. They don’t look as evocative as those you saw at Halloran’s Haberdashery, but you have a feeling these are also less cursed. Still, you don’t feel any desire to put one on.
