Besides your echoing footsteps and the gasps of leaking pressure tubes, the winding alley behind the machine shop is eerie and quiet. If it weren’t for his shining, silver eyes, Archer calm, visionary might well be mistaken for a human man.

Yet, our minds are still not our own: many of us struggle to recall basic memories from our old lives. The key to our restoration lies in the first Unchained. He is imprisoned below Sparkwright Tower—a place many of us once served. He belongs here with his family, not in a cage.
