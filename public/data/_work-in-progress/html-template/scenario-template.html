Charterhall's main thoroughfare is quiet by the time you meet with BASIL (ruthless, sweaty), owner of the aptly named and aptly spacious 'Endless Apothecary', where he proudly declares if a herb or remedy can't be found here, it doesn't exist. Basil leads you through his lengthy store before rummaging through a binder of leaflets before handing you one. The leaflet reads: ‘Big Announcement! GOLDEN BUTTON MUSHROOMS are now available at the Golden Harvest Grotto in Barrowcleft. Heal any wound for a small contribution. We do require that our miracle mushroom be consumed on-premises. Signed, Ravena Glenmont’ “This came to my attention only last week, and I wouldn't have believed it if I hadn't gone to Barrowcleft and seen it for myself! Get me a sample of the stuff, and make sure they can’t make any more. I'll be able to charge whatever I want when I’m the only source!”
