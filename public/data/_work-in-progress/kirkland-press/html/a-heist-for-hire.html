Lord Harrow’s study in Brightstone is cold and spacious, lit by buzzing electric lights. Leather-bound books on antique shelves sit alongside Tycherosi artifacts and jars of shimmering liquids. He has made a name for himself as a collector of oddities, both magical and mundane.

I have summoned each of you here today because you come highly recommended. Within the vault of Charterhall Bank is an item I require—a gemstone called the Sable Eye. You will retrieve it for me. I was able to acquire the bank’s layout, let’s go over it together.
